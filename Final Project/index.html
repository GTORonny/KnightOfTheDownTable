making_bank

By Aaron Webb

I created this story because I've sorta fallen in love with the idea of telling stories through programming. When I made my we development assignment it was frustrating but the actually creative part were incredibly fun. Their isn't a political or emotional reason that I created this. I mostly just wanted to immerse someone in a interesting story for however long they decide to stay on the page.

Their plan was perfect.

The team was assembled.

The goal was clear.

They knew the layout of the place.

They got in.

Were prepared for anything.

Than left without anyone knowing they were there.

THE_END
